About

How did I get here?

  • 2011-2014

    IESB

    I started my Advertising and marketing degree at IESB. The course took 8 semesters and I finished it at the end of 2014.

  • 2012-2014

    English Teacher - Spanish Idomas

    From March 2012 to March 2014 I worked as English Teacher at Spanish Idiomas, a language school situated at Brasilia, Brazil. This was my first experience as an English Teacher so I learned a lot. I could develop my speaking, my vocabulary, my grammar knowledge, my listening and other skills like public speech, my organization to plan the classes, my creativity to have dynamic classes, I learned how to work with different profiles of people.

  • March 2014 - September 2014

    Social Media Internship - Vila do Pequenino Jesus

    In March 2014 I had the pleasure to work in the philanthropic association Vila do Pequenino Jesus situated at Brasilia, Brazil.Their goal is to give a dignified life for people with multiple disabilities. My job was to manage their social networks like Facebook page and their site. The main goal was to attract people interested in helping with donations or something else. At Vila do Pequenino Jesus I could develop myself as a person,I could see people with a lot of problems happy, smiling, and grateful. Today I am a better person after this experience..

  • August 2015 - December 2019

    English Teacher - School Nossa Senhora Aparecida COC

    From 2014 to 2019 I worked as an English Teacher for kids and teenagers in a private school. This job helped me to become a more organized person, that's why I always had too many students for different ages and levels. Working in a private school made me a more political person, I had to develop skills to face different situations with students, their parents and the school administration.

  • March 2020

    Private English Teacher

    From 2020 I am working as a private English Teacher. Working with kids, teenagers and adults. This type of class is different, I have to talk to the students to understand their goals, why are they studying English.

  • June 2020

    First life change decision - Lose weight

    During the Covid-19 pandemic I made the decision to lose weight. In the first appointment with a nutritionist I weighed 140kg. Today I lost more than 50kg and this was a real life change. I can sleep better, I feel better with myself, I can do things I couldn't and I could know my new passion…

  • August 2021

    Second life change decision - Become a parachutist

    During my weight loss my brother gave me a challenge. If I got to 115kg, he would take me skydiving. In November 2020 I got there and I had my first experience with skydiving, a tandem skydive. In August 2021 I did a course to become a parachutist. The sport became my new passion and I realized I should look for ways to get extra money to keep skydiving.

  • First half of 2022

    Studying programming by myself.

    I saw in programming a chance to make an extra income to keep skydiving. Then I started to study with some free courses. I did a Python course at Curso em vídeo and HTML, CSS and Javascript courses at Origamid. During my studies I realized I was enjoying programming and I saw this could be my main profession so I decided to invest more time and effort to make this happen.

  • August 2022 - October 2022

    Ironhack's full time Web Development bootcamp

    When I saw programming could be my profession I decided to do a bootcamp at Ironhack. In two months I learned a lot of things, like Javascript, HTML, CSS, React.js, Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB and Rest api. During the course I did three projects using all the technologies I had learned. I am sure in these two months I could learn what would take at least one year studying by myself. After the bootcamp I feel prepared to assume a position as Web Developer.

Game Tasting

Fullstack application done during module 3 of Ironhack's full time Web development bootcamp

Previous Next


This project was the third and the final one made during Ironhack's Web Development bootcamp. After 9 weeks we could develop a full stack application. The API was developed using NodeJS and MongoDB. For the front-end we used ReactJS, formik, bootstrap, React Hot Toast, all based in JavaScript, HTML and CSS.

This is a website for game developers and for who likes to test new games. If you are a game developer you have the chance to show your game under development, and receive feedback from other users, this way you can improve it. If you enjoy testing games, feel free to test, give feedback and like the games. If you really liked you can add to your favorites and follow its development.
